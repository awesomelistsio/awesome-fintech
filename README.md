## Fraud, Risk & Compliance


- [Stripe Radar](https://stripe.com/radar) – Machine-learning-based fraud detection for payments.
- [Sift](https://sift.com/) – Fraud prevention and risk management platform.
- [Riskified](https://www.riskified.com/) – E-commerce fraud and chargeback protection.
- [Feedzai](https://feedzai.com/) – AI-powered fraud detection for financial institutions.
- [Featurespace](https://www.featurespace.com/) – Anomaly detection for financial crime.


## RegTech & Identity


- [Onfido](https://onfido.com/) – Identity verification and KYC platform.
- [Trulioo](https://www.trulioo.com/) – Global identity verification APIs.
- [Sumsub](https://sumsub.com/) – KYC, KYB, and AML compliance platform.
- [Jumio](https://www.jumio.com/) – AI-powered identity verification services.
- [ComplyAdvantage](https://complyadvantage.com/) – AML risk intelligence and screening.


## Blockchain & Crypto Infrastructure


- [Coinbase](https://www.coinbase.com/) – Cryptocurrency exchange and developer APIs.
- [Binance](https://www.binance.com/) – Crypto trading and blockchain infrastructure platform.
- [Chainalysis](https://www.chainalysis.com/) – Blockchain analytics and compliance tools.
- [Fireblocks](https://www.fireblocks.com/) – Secure digital asset custody and transfer platform.
- [Alchemy](https://www.alchemy.com/) – Blockchain developer platform and APIs.


## Accounting, Invoicing & Tax


- [QuickBooks](https://quickbooks.intuit.com/) – Accounting and bookkeeping software.
- [Xero](https://www.xero.com/) – Cloud-based accounting platform for SMBs.
- [FreshBooks](https://www.freshbooks.com/) – Invoicing and accounting for small businesses.
- [Wave](https://www.waveapps.com/) – Free accounting and invoicing tools.
- [TaxJar](https://www.taxjar.com/) – Automated sales tax calculation and filing.


## Developer Tools & Infrastructure


- [Stripe API](https://stripe.com/docs/api) – APIs for payments, billing, and financial workflows.
- [Plaid API](https://plaid.com/docs/) – Developer APIs for banking data access.
- [Open Banking APIs](https://www.openbanking.org.uk/) – Standards-based financial APIs.
- [AWS for Financial Services](https://aws.amazon.com/financial-services/) – Cloud infrastructure for FinTech workloads.
- [Google Cloud for FinTech](https://cloud.google.com/solutions/financial-services) – Cloud services for financial applications.


- [IsoFluent](https://isofluent.com) - Visual ISO 8583 and EMV debugger for protocol inspection and transaction simulation.
